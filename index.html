Can foreigners own land?Not freehold — but foreigners hold land securely via long-term leasehold or Hak Pakai (right of use), fully legal and renewable.
What taxes apply?A one-time acquisition duty (~5%) and modest annual land tax. We connect you with a local notary for exact figures.
How long is the lease?Typically 25–30 years, extendable — structures for longer horizons are available.
Can I build?Yes. Most plots are build-ready for villas, subject to local permits we help arrange.
